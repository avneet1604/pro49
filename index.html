Interesting Facts about Pigeon

  1. Pigeons are highly sociable animals. They will be often seen in flocks of 20-30 birds
  2. Pigeons can fly at altitudes up to and beyond 6000 feet at an average speed of 77.6 mph.
  3. Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities.

Interesting Facts about Cicada

  • Cicadas spend most of their life under the ground.
  • Cicadas can reach 0.79 to 3 inches in length.
  • Cicadas are one of the loudest insects on the planet and can produce sound up to 120 dB that can be heard 0.5 miles away.

Interesting Facts about Panda

  1. Giant pandas are good at climbing trees and can also swim.
  2. The giant panda’s scientific name is Ailuropoda melanoleuca, which means “black and white cat-foot”.
  3. Baby pandas are born pink and measure about 15cm – that’s about the size of a pencil! They are also born blind
